Spaghetti with Garlic Bread
My family was gone for a week on vacation to Hawaii (without me so sad) so my boyfriend made a huge batch of spaghetti for me to eat throughout the week! Homemade spaghetti is super simple and tastes so much better than store-bought marinara jars.
We headed to Trader Joe's because who doesn't love that place? All those ingredients shown above came out to about $20 and this lasted me at least 7 meals.
While my boyfriend was making that perfect homemade marinara, I wanted to make myself useful and whip up some garlic butter to make garlic bread. It was a safe food because what can go wrong with making garlic bread? Just kidding, apparently my garlic bread wasn't very garlicky BUT at least the ciabatta had a nice crunch to it.
