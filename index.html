Welcome to the website of Theta Tau, Omicron Gamma Chapter! We take pride in being the only co-ed professional engineering fraternity at UC Davis, as well as the first engineering fraternity of the nation. We consistently strive to prepare ourselves for a better future, to create events for the good of the entire student body, and to strengthen the bonds within our organization - through our three pillars of professionalism, community service and brotherhood.
