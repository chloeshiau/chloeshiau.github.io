Chubsy's Kitchen
Oatmeal Chocolate Chip Cookies
I love adding oatmeal into my cookies because of its texture and it's healthier than using all flour! For chocolate chip cookies, I often like to use chocolate chunks - it just tastes better to me even though I'm not sure if there's a difference besides shape from chocolate chips
I leave my dough in the fridge for about 1-2 hours because it makes the perfect cookies - it won't come out too puffy or too flat.
