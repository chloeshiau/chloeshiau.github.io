Monterey, CA
I have met the best of friends in college, a couple of them being my pledge brothers. We've seen the best and the worst of each other, and we've been inseperable since freshman year! In December, one of them was to move to Seattle permanently and it was heartbreaking, yet a very huge life step for her and I am so proud - so we had to have a last hoorah before she left. Right after their finals, we headed to Monterey for a mini vacation.
We came to Monterey pretty early and it wasn't time to check-in to our Airbnbs to be lazy yet, so we headed to the 17-mile drive to see some coastal views. We also shot some go-pro videos - not your normal ones though so do not take this video seriously!
The next day, our Airbnb host gave us guest passes to Monterey Bay Aquarium so of course we had to check it out. We felt like little kids again as we strolled through the aquarium touching starfishes, watching sea otters do tricks, and watching the staff feed sea anemones.
If you've heard that your best friends in life are often found in college, then you've heard correctly. In college, you'll have the best and worst moments in your life and I wouldn't want to share them (or cry) with anyone else but these lovely ladies.
